### Using Unsplash Images:
1. Click the Unsplash link
2. Click the green "Download" button (requires free account)
3. Images are free for commercial use
4. Attribution appreciated but not required

### Using Pexels Images:
1. Click the Pexels link
2. Click "Free Download" and select size
3. Completely free, no attribution required

### Image Specifications:
- **Hero Slides:** 1920x1080px minimum (landscape)
- **Service Images:** 800x600px minimum  
- **Team Photos:** 400x400px (square, portrait orientation)
- **Blog Thumbnails:** 800x500px (landscape)
